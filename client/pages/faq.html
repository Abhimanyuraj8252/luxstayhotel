FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is from 3:00 PM, and check-out is until 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability.

Is breakfast included in the room rate?

We offer various rate packages. Some include our signature breakfast buffet, while others are room-only. Please check your booking details.

Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes, we provide private luxury transfers to and from the airport for an additional fee. Please contact our concierge to arrange this service.

Is the pool heated?

Yes, our outdoor infinity pool is temperature-controlled year-round for your comfort.
