A Landmark of Luxury

Founded over a century ago, LuxeStay has been a beacon of hospitality for discerning travelers from around the globe. What started as a boutique inn has evolved into a world-renowned destination, preserving its historical charm while embracing modern sophistication.

Our philosophy is simple: to provide an oasis of calm where every need is anticipated and every desire is met with a smile. From our award-winning architecture to our Michelin-starred culinary experiences, we invite you to be part of our legacy.
